Rescue Coordination Application
Overview
The Rescue Coordination Application is a centralized platform that aims to streamline and enhance the coordination efforts of various rescue and relief agencies during natural or man-made calamities. The application allows registered rescue agencies to share and access real-time location data, facilitating better collaboration and response management.

Features
Agency Registration: Rescue agencies can register and create profiles, providing essential information about their capabilities, resources, and areas of expertise.

Real-time Location Sharing: Agencies can share their current locations in real-time, allowing others to visualize and coordinate efforts effectively.

Calamity Dashboard: A centralized dashboard displays information about ongoing calamities, including their nature, severity, and affected regions.

Resource Inventory: Agencies can update and share their available resources such as personnel, vehicles, and equipment, aiding in resource allocation and optimization.

Communication Hub: Built-in communication tools enable secure and instant messaging between registered agencies, fostering better collaboration.
